About Virtutissima and its purpose

This website shall serve those who seek a more intensive way of living. Either you are looking for self-improvement or you are just trying to make sense of the harsh world you are thrown into, although you are weak and imperfect and don't feel up to the job of surviving in human societal contexts and in unforgiving nature itself. Either way I hope this website will help you to fulfill your human potential as best as you can while at the same time it certainly will help me to fulfill mine myself.

As we are weak and ignorant beings who think highly of themselves, I wish us all the luck the universe might grant to learn to accept our infirmity and to rise in spite. So join me on the path of virtue to reach a higher state of being that is more connected to this impure world. Because even the wrong and twisted (or better: those alone) will learn to see and to rid themselves from the impoverishment of the soul.

But be aware: Non est ad astra molis e terris via. The way from the earth to the stars is not easy, as Seneca put it.
